www.TweetPNG.com

www.TweetPNG.com is a simple API to get a tweet as a PNG!

Example

Add https://www.tweetpng.com/:username/tweet/last.png to your img tag in your HTML, where :username is your Twitter handle.

Add https://www.tweetpng.com/:username/tweet/:tweetID.png to your img tag in your HTML, where :username is your Twitter handle and :tweetID in the tweet ID.
